    About Living Documents

    The "Living Documents" feature makes documents of the past to come to life with sound, transcripts, and informational pop-ups. Created by MSE Fellows and Mystic Seaport staff, Living Documents contain teacher-friendly components such as Questions for Deeper Thought, Artifact Facts, Related Items from the Collection, Related Resources, In the Classroom ideas and Related Websites.
