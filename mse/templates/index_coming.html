The Mystic Seaport for Educators Website

A community of practice among educators, students, and museum staff, the Mystic Seaport for Educators (MSE) represents a shift from delivering content in a static, one-way manner to a more participatory learning model that fosters shared authority, merging the expertise of museum staff with the standards-based needs of classroom educators. In the MSE research and planning phase, Mystic Seaport worked with 140 educators and students in a series of co-creation workshops. Their ideas and feedback have shaped the development of the Mystic Seaport for Educators Website, set to launch November 6, 2013.

The new website will feature: artifact articles, living manuscripts, active maps, thematic resource sets, educator profiles and projects, online lectures, scholar interviews and more!
