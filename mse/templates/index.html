Our MSE Community of Learners

The MSE Community includes educators, families and students involved in Mystic Seaport projects, partnerships and website content creation. Click on "Community Projects" or "Community Members" to learn more about individuals and their classroom or web-based projects.

About This Project

This website is an ongoing collaboration between Mystic Seaport and a growing community of educators. Working together, we aim to bring the treasures and resources of Mystic Seaport out of the Museum and into classrooms and homes across the country. Please check back in the future, as we continue to add new content regularly. More...
