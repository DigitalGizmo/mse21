Mystic Seaport for Educators

Online Resources • Enhanced Collection Access • Community of Learners

  • Artifact
    Articles

    Brief information and context about an artifact in the collection. Includes questions for deeper thought and other classroom ideas.
  • Living Manuscripts

    Manuscripts of the past come to life with sound, transcripts, magic lens and informational pop-ups.
  • Active Maps


    Geospatial representations of objects and manuscripts from our collection. Includes "stories" feature and classroom ideas.
  • Resource
    Sets

    Collections of OLC website features (artifact articles, living manuscripts, maps, lectures and interviews) that are tied together under a central theme
  • Lectures & Scholars

    Audio recordings of popular lectures at the Munson Institute of Mystic Seaport, as well as OLC teacher-scholar interviews.

About This Project

This website is an ongoing collaboration between Mystic Seaport Museum and a growing community of teachers. Working together, we aim to bring the treasures of Mystic Seaport Museum's vast collection out of the museum and into classrooms and homes. Learn more about this project
